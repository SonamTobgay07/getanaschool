GETANA PRIMARY SCHOOL

The school is located eighty two Kilometer away from Gedu town where climate
condition is moderated, making learning environment suitable.
The school was established on year 2000 and has been sustained
for twenty four years with improvements in teaching learning. The school acquires all kind of facilities including internet connectivivty, ICT equipments, better infrastructures and well skilled teachers. Furthermore, students
performances in acadamic excel maintaining reputation of school.
The school consists of supportives community, consistent, safe learning environment, better resources and commitments to extra curricular activities.

SCHOOL KNOWN FOR;

1. Teachers throughly conversant with modern development.
2. Quality of teaching
3. Focus on student welfare
4. Academic standards
5. School leadership
6. Facilities and resources
7. School reputation

VISION
An educated and enlightened society of GNH, built and sustained on the unique
Bhutanese values of tha dam-tsig ley gju-drey.
MISSION
Equip all children with appropriate knowledge, skills and values to cope with the challenges of the 21st century.
